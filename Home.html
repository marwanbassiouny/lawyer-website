introduction:

Zayan & Associates Law Office is An Egyptian Law Office who works according to the prevailing Egyptian laws and aims to provide Legal services for international and local authorities, institutions, companies and for individuals And that in various local fields in accordance with local and international laws in the Arab Republic of Egypt.
The Partners and members of the Firm are senior professionals with years of experience behind them. They bring the highest level of professional service to clients along with the traditions of the profession, integrity and sound ethical practices. Members of the Firm are in tune with the work-culture of international law firms as well as the expectations of large corporate clients.
